My Journey Into Life Insurance

My name is Taiwo Folorunsho, and my journey into the life insurance industry started with a simple realization: people deserve to understand how money and protection truly work.

In my experience working with families across Canada, I noticed a pattern — most people were only introduced to basic term life insurance because it is easy to sell and easy to buy. But very few were shown the full picture.

I didn’t join this industry just to sell policies. I joined to educate, empower, and reveal the opportunities that many people don’t even know exist.

We are living longer than ever before. That means there is a powerful opportunity to not only protect your family, but also benefit from your policy while you're alive — through tax-efficient growth, living benefits, and strategic planning.

Since starting my journey, I have helped families secure their income, protect their futures, and gain confidence in their financial path.

And this is just the beginning.

Comprehensive Protection & Growth Strategies

We design solutions that protect what matters most today while building wealth you can access tomorrow.

Whole Life

Permanent coverage with guaranteed cash value growth. Build tax-efficient wealth while protecting your family for life.

Term Life

Affordable temporary protection for specific periods. Ideal for mortgage protection, income replacement, and young families.

Universal Life

Flexible permanent insurance with investment options. Adjust premiums and coverage as your life and goals evolve.

Critical Illness

Lump-sum benefit if diagnosed with covered conditions. Focus on recovery, not finances, during challenging times.

Disability Insurance

Protect your income if illness or injury prevents you from working. Your most valuable asset deserves protection.

Estate Planning

Preserve your legacy and minimize taxes. Ensure wealth transfers efficiently to the next generation.

Group Benefits

Comprehensive employee benefit packages. Attract and retain top talent while providing valuable protection.
